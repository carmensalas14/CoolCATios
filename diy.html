Window Box DIY




The WINDOW BOX DIY Catio Plan by Catio Spaces is a 3-sided catio that provides fresh air and a bird's eye view for your cat to safely enjoy the enrichment of the outdoors. Designed to complement your home, the Window Box catio can be painted or left natural. The Window Box is ideal for sash or slider windows and the plans include instructions for angle braces and a polycarbonate or wire mesh roof option. (The Window Box catio can also be mounted completely under the window sill with cat access through a wall mounted cat door). Plans are a PDF download.

*DIY Catio Plans are available as a PDF DOWNLOAD. Materials are not included.


Haven DIY




The HAVEN DIY Catio Plan by Catio Spaces is a compact, 3-sided catio ideal for a garden, patio or deck. If you have limited space, the Haven maximizes vertical height with shelves and perches for your cat's enjoyment. Catio height allows cat access through a window or door. Available in 3x6 and 4x8 catio plan sizes that feature a polycarbonate or wire mesh roof option. Plans are a PDF download.

About Us
